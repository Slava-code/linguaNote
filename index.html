🌐 Introducing LinguaNote

"The same note-taking you love — now in every language, with real-time caption support."

LinguaNote extends the current experience by layering in AI-powered translation and real-time captioning, making it the first true multilingual notetaking assistant that works across Zoom, Meet, Teams, and any desktop workflow — with zero disruption.

🧩 Feature 1: Real-Time Caption + Translation Layer

Understand what's said — live, in your language.

LinguaNote runs a subtle overlay on your desktop that listens to the same audio other apps are processing, and displays:

  • ✅ Real-time captions of your meeting
  • 🌐 Translated text (live) in your preferred language
  • 🧠 Smart rephrasing options (e.g. simple English, formal French, academic tone)

"You don't just take notes — you see what's being said, in the language you need."

🔁 Feature 2: Translated Summaries, Notes, and Context Chat

Everything note-taking apps do — now multilingual.

With LinguaNote enabled, the entire note lifecycle is translation-aware:

  • Your typed notes are translated into your chosen language
  • Summaries are auto-generated and localized
  • Multi-meeting context chat can be queried in any language

Imagine asking:

"En español: ¿Qué se discutió sobre el diseño de producto en las últimas tres reuniones?"

And getting a perfect answer, sourced from your real notes — with citations.
